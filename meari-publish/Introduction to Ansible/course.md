A hands-on beginner's course in **Ansible**, the agentless IT-automation tool. It starts from "what problem does Ansible solve?" and builds up to writing real, idempotent playbooks: inventory, ad-hoc commands, tasks and modules, variables and facts, templates, handlers, roles, and securing secrets with Vault. By the end you can automate the setup and configuration of servers in a repeatable, version-controlled way.
